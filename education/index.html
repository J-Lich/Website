I commenced my studies in the field of Electrical & Electronic Engineering with a second Major in Mathematical Computer Sciences in March 2015. I instantly knew it was the right choice for me. Understanding the core functionality of electronics and code is imperative to troubleshooting issues or developing from the ground up. Acquiring this complete understanding of complex systems excited me more than I ever thought possible and Adelaide University has provided me with a taste of a number of different topics to develop a list of fascinations and interests. While developing a thorough understanding of the fundamentals, university resources have enabled me to experience a wide range of tools and equipment which I may require in any future role.

I undertook an international exchange with the Global Learning Society from September 2017 through until April 2018. This experience took me to British Columbia (Canada) where I continued my studies in Engineering and Computer Sciences from a different perspective, while taking some time time travel the world and connect with new people.

From reception (2002) through to year 12 (2014) Mercedes College was the home of my education. This is where I acquired a large component of my study habits, knowledge base, and most importantly my core values. I partook in community events, sporting and educational related extra curricular activities. I maintained connection with he school for several years, returning for Maths, Physics and Economics tutoring, as well as High Jump coaching.
