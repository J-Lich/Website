Who am I?

An Electrical & Electronic Engineering graduate with an ever expanding passion for electronics. My interests span across battery technologies, the sustainable energy market, Electric Vehicles and basic circuitry. I constantly keep myself occupied with self directed projects to expand my knowledge and hands on experience, as well as spark inspiration.

What are my projects?

Any project that peaks my interest or gets me thinking! An Electric Vehicle theme in my projects has emerged, starting with a self built electric skateboard to get from A to B, to developing an electric bike and finally to working on an electric car conversion for my final year project and thesis! I also run an Ubuntu Server that hosts all of my automated online needs, including this Apache webpage which was coded from scratch (HTML, JavaScript and CSS). All examples of my passion for putting theory to practice in the real world.

What do I have to offer?

With my professional customer service and IT experience; higher level education; and my ever-growing list of DIY projects, my determined nature enables me to quickly adapt, learn, and enhance my technical abilities in a flexible and professional manner. My experience in management/documentation of projects, allows me to provide stability, while my passion and enthusiasm pushes the potential limits of any project.
